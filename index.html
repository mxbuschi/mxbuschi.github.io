Maximilian Busch

I am a Technical Consultant at MathWorks. I advise and support development teams in best practices of Model-Based Design using MATLAB/Simulink.

Formerly, I was a PhD student and research associate at the Institute for Machine Tools and Industrial Management (iwb) at the Technical University of Munich.

My research was focused on

🎯 accuracy improvement of robot-based milling processes,

🛠️ information fusion of data-driven models and physics-based structural models using probabilistic machine learning methods and

☁ smart data analytics and information security in cloud-based data processing of OPC UA machine tool data.
